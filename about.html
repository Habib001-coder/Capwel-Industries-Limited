Capwel Industries Limited

Capwel Industries Limited is a leading producer of wheat flour ,maize flour,Rice and even Yola juice in Kenya,
including well known brands such as Soko, Amaize,Ranne and Yola .

Who We Are

We are a family owned enterprise
located in Thika town.

Established in 1990,
Capwel Industries Limited is one of the oldest flour miller in Kenya.
We are very proud of our place among Kenyan Grain Millers because,
we play an important role in ensuring the Kenyan market enjoys high quality wheat,
maize flour for local needs.

We produce the Capwel brand
that includes well known brands such as
Soko maize flour,Soko Wheat flour,Yola,Soko porridge flour,Spaggetti and Ranne.

What We Do

Our main production plant is located
three kilometers from the Thika
super-highway, just off Garissa Rd.
Our location makes it easy
for us to serve customers in different parts of Kenya.
In addition,
we have well developed distribution lines that run
deep into the Central region and the Rift Valley,
and all the regions in between.
